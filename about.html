About Me

Hello! My name is Zac Callahan, an amateur composer and musician passionate about all things music. With a background in music performance, I like to spend my spare time performing and composing orchestral pieces.

I am proficient in playing guitar and trumpet, but also dabble in piano. When I'm not playing on my own, I am an active member of the Monash Philharmonic Orchestra where I play first Trumpet.

My biggest musical inspirations are Beethoven, Mahler, John Williams, Howard Shore, Hans Zimmer, John Powell, Marty O'Donnell just to name a few! Thanks for visiting — feel free to explore a selection of my work on this site, or get in touch!
