About Me

Hello! I'm Zac Callahan, a composer and musician passionate about crafting expressive and immersive soundscapes. With a background in classical training and modern composition, I blend tradition with innovation to create music that tells a story.

Whether I'm scoring for film, writing for ensembles, or experimenting with new textures in electronic sound, my goal is always to evoke emotion and connection through music. I’ve worked with orchestras, collaborated on multimedia projects, and released a variety of original compositions.

When I'm not composing, I enjoy exploring nature, discovering new music, and collaborating with fellow creatives. Thanks for visiting—feel free to explore my work, or get in touch!
